Class 9 | Ch1: Number Systems | Exercise 1.6

Q1. Find: (i) 64^(1/2)  (ii) 32^(1/5)  (iii) 125^(1/3)
Answer: (i) вҲҡ64=8. (ii) (2вҒө)^(1/5)=2. (iii) (5Ві)^(1/3)=5. вң… Rule: a^(1/n) = nth root of a.

Q2. Find: (i) 9^(3/2)  (ii) 32^(2/5)  (iii) 16^(3/4)  (iv) 125^(-1/3)
Answer: (i) (вҲҡ9)Ві=3Ві=27. (ii) (32^(1/5))ВІ=2ВІ=4. (iii) (16^(1/4))Ві=2Ві=8. (iv) 1/(125^(1/3))=1/5. вң… Rule: a^(m/n)=(a^(1/n))^m.
